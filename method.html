Create the Sauce

First, we must gather and prepare the ingrededients. We can make a curry paste by rough chopping the garlic, ginger and shallots.
Add them into a blender or food processor and create a coarse paste. Remove from blender and set aside.
Add the Canned Tomato to the blender or food processor and create a coarse paste. Remove from blender and strain. Retain liquid.
Heat a pan and melt the clarified butter. Brown the meat in clarified butter.
Remove the meat from the pan and retain any rendered fat. Set aside meat and begin creating the sauce.
Add Whole Cinnamon, Clove, Black Cardamom, Coriander, Mustard Seed and Bay Leaf to ghee. Heat spices in oil until the mustard seed begins to sizzle.
Add curry paste to oil and fry under low heat until ingrededients begin to caramelize.
Add Chopped tomato and fry under low heat until tomatoes begin to dry.
Add Add Cashews, Cumin Powder, Turmeric Powder, Chilli Powder and fry until fragrant.
Add Tomato Juice and simmer until juice no longer tastes raw.
Remove ingredients from pan and carefully place inside blender. Blend until smooth, thinning the paste with coconut milk.
Strain through sieve and discard the grit.
Add salt and sugar to taste.

Using the Sauce

Brown meat and add sauce. Simmer until tender. To finish sauce, season with Garam Masala and Kasoori Methi. Fold in Yogurt and sprinkle Chopped Fresh Cilantro on top.
